About Us

Welcome to John Oteivbi Otabor Foundation (JOOF) Established since 2020

In 2020, the John Oteivbi Otabor Foundation was established in loving memory of the late John Oteivbi Otabor, a respected former Assistant Commissioner of Police who passed away in 1974. The foundation was founded by his daughter, Vera Verissimo (Nee Otabor), as a tribute to her fatherâ€™s enduring legacy of service and compassion.

The foundation's mission is to support less privileged children and youth from extremely poor backgrounds, providing them with opportunities and hope for a brighter future. Through a variety of programs and initiatives, the foundation aims to address the educational, emotional, and developmental needs of these young individuals.
The John Oteivbi Otabor Foundation is driven by a commitment to honor the values and dedication of its namesake, ensuring that his legacy of public service continues to make a positive impact on the lives of those who need it most.
